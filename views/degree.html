Core Requirements

These are the courses offered by the department of your major that are necessary to complete in order to obtain the degree in this discipline from your University.

Breadth Requirements

Breadth courses are those that are offered by other departments within the school of your major, in order to broaden your knowledge. The listings below show which courses can be taken to fulfil this requirement.

Language Requirements

These are language courses (French, German, Spanish, Italian etc.) offered by the Liberal Arts Dept. of your University, two of which must be taken to fulfil this requirement. Refer to the listing below.

General Elective Requirements
